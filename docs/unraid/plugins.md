title: Plugins
description: List of Unraid Plugins maintained by EDAC.
---

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

- :simple-tailscale: __Tailscale__

    ---
    A VPN service that makes it easy to connect your Unraid server to other devices securely over the internet.

    :fontawesome-solid-circle-info: [Documentation](tailscale/plugin.md)

    :fontawesome-brands-github: [Repository](https://github.com/unraid/unraid-tailscale)

- :simple-tailscale: __Tailscale IDP (tsidp)__

    ---
    Easy and secure authentication to the Unraid WebGUI using Tailscale.

    :fontawesome-solid-circle-info: [Documentation](tailscale/tsidp.md)

    :fontawesome-brands-github: [Repository](https://github.com/dkaser/unraid-tsidp)

- :fontawesome-solid-lock: __Automatic Disk Unlock__

    ---
    Automatically unlocks your encrypted array and encrypted disks at boot time using Shamir's Secret Sharing to protect your disk encryption key.

    :fontawesome-solid-circle-info: [Documentation](auto-unlock/setup.md)

    :fontawesome-brands-github: [Repository](https://github.com/dkaser/unraid-auto-unlock)

- :fontawesome-solid-desktop: __MOTD__

    ---
    Display system information (CPU, memory, disk usage, service status, etc.) after SSH/console login with customizable layout and warnings.

    :fontawesome-brands-github: [Repository](https://github.com/dkaser/unraid-motd)

- :fontawesome-solid-stethoscope: __Plugin Diagnostics__

    ---
    Generate and download diagnostic files for installed plugins to help with troubleshooting and support.

    :fontawesome-solid-circle-info: [Documentation](plugin-diagnostics/usage.md)

    :fontawesome-brands-github: [Repository](https://github.com/dkaser/unraid-plugin-diagnostics)

- :fontawesome-solid-tags: __Label Manager__

    ---
    Easily apply container labels for services like TSDProxy and SWAG through a user-friendly WebGUI interface.

    :fontawesome-brands-github: [Repository](https://github.com/dkaser/unraid-labelman)

- :fontawesome-solid-train: __Steam Locomotive (sl)__

    ---
    By special request on the Unraid Discord: cure your bad habit of mistyping. A fun easter egg command.

    :fontawesome-brands-github: [Repository](https://github.com/dkaser/unraid-sl)

- :fontawesome-solid-align-left: __Enhanced Log v2__

    ---
    Enhanced syslog viewer with highlighted lines, color customization, and advanced filtering capabilities for easier log analysis.

    :fontawesome-brands-github: [Repository](https://github.com/dkaser/unraid-enhancedlog)

- :fontawesome-solid-folder: __File Activity v2__

    ---
    Monitor recent open/write/modify file activity on each disk, Unassigned Devices, and cache/pool devices to understand why disks are spinning up.

    :fontawesome-brands-github: [Repository](https://github.com/dkaser/unraid-fileactivity)

- :fontawesome-solid-folder-open: __Open Files v2__

    ---
    Monitor processes with open files on the array that might prevent clean shutdown, with the ability to stop problematic processes.

    :fontawesome-brands-github: [Repository](https://github.com/dkaser/unraid-openfiles)

- :fontawesome-solid-hdd: __ncdu__

    ---
    A disk usage analyzer with a text-mode user interface for finding what's consuming disk space.

    :fontawesome-brands-github: [Repository](https://github.com/dkaser/unraid-ncdu)

- :fontawesome-solid-life-ring: __ddrescue__

    ---
    GNU ddrescue is a data recovery tool that copies data from one file or block device to another, trying to rescue the good parts first in case of read errors.

    :fontawesome-brands-github: [Repository](https://github.com/dkaser/unraid-ddrescue)

- :fontawesome-solid-microchip: __storcli64__

    ---
    Command-line utility for managing storage controllers.

    :fontawesome-brands-github: [Repository](https://github.com/dkaser/unraid-storcli)


</div>
